Build credibility with Verified SMS

What is Verified SMS?

Businesses share information with customers using SMS- Account Alerts, Reminders, Order Updates and many more.
But when a customer receives SMS from an unknown Sender ID it becomes difficult for them to trust the sender and messages.
With Verified SMS build that trust by adding your brand name and business logo along with the verified status
to all your business messages and let your customers know they are receiving legitimate and spam free messages.
This will help brands like yours to increase interactions and brand visibility.

How does Verified SMS benefit your business?

Branding

Aid brand recognition with the brand name and
brand logo. Enhance your brand global scalability.

Trust

Sender verification helps build trust in your
communication.

Spam Detection

Protecting you from spam by warning you of unsafe
content. SMS verification helps your business to limit
spam accounts, fight against fraud.

Secure

All communications between business and customer are secure. Apply 2-factor authentication for adding more security.

Engaging

When a customer knows who’s texting, the
engagement increases, which also helps optimize
conversion rates.
